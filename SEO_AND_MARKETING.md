## 3. ON-PAGE SEO REQUIREMENTS

### Meta Tags (Already Implemented)

**Homepage:**
```html
<title>Rehab Source | Home Modifications Assessment Portal for OTs</title>
<meta name="description" content="Australia's leading digital platform for Occupational Therapists conducting NDIS home modification assessments. Streamline your workflow from assessment to funding application." />
```

### Heading Structure

Every page MUST have:
- **One H1 tag** - Main page topic
- **Logical H2-H6 hierarchy** - Subsections organized logically
- **Keywords in headings** - Natural placement of target keywords

### Image Optimization

All images MUST have:
- **Descriptive alt text** - Explain what the image shows
- **Compressed file size** - Under 100KB for UI images
- **WebP format** - With PNG/JPG fallback
- **Lazy loading** - For below-fold images

### Internal Linking

- Link to related pages naturally within content
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- Ensure no orphan pages (all pages reachable from navigation)
- Maximum 3 clicks from homepage to any page
